Who we are

We are agricultural solution providers, we identify the problems and fix them with our  tailor made solutions.

The unstructured nature of the agric sector has prevented its growth for decades. Production, aggregation, Processing and financing are key processes that are involved in the sector from farm to table. These are cumbersome processes that require so many moving parts. Our involvement over the past years has revealed significant gaps such as lack of visibility,, risky/unsecure transactions, lack of information, financial exclusion, limited credible data management systems and a lack of accountability amongst others.This has greatly limited private sector involvement, the industrialization and involvement of  development agencies, financial institutions and even government bodies from establishing sustainable structures towards improving the livelihood of local agripreneurs, farmers and farming communities. This is why our purpose in Agriarche is to identify these critical pain points and collaborate with strategic partners through our technology and network to connect farmers, aggregators, agripreneurs to services such as markets, finance, advisory, mechanization, insurance, financial inclusion, storage,training and other required services. We are set on a mission to better the lives of farmers, agripreneurs and all actors taking one community, state at a time.
